Welcome to CBI @ Matchat!

How many opportunities at conferences are lost forever because the people involved did not notice them? We're here to help!

Here, you post your needs, wishes, interests, offers - one by one. When they are matched with others, you can chat with them and see if it's one of these opportunities.

To get started, most users post about what they would like to do in the conference after-hours, others jump right in and offer a PhD position.

Up to you!
